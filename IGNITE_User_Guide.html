2. Logging In

Who Needs to Log In?

  • Super Admins (event organizers)
  • Committee Members (registration, jury, food, logistics)
  • Teams registering for an event (Google sign-in required before filling the form)

Note: Team Portal access (/team) does NOT require login ‚Äî anyone can check their team using phone number or team code.

How to Log In

  1. Look at the top-right corner of the page
  2. Click the blue "Staff Login" button
  3. A Google sign-in window opens
  4. Select your Google account (must be the same email the Super Admin added for your role)
  5. After sign-in, you return to the website
  6. Your name and profile photo now appear in the top-right corner
  7. A "Dashboard" link appears in the navbar ‚Äî click it to go to your dashboard

How to Log Out

  1. Click the gray "Logout" button in the top-right corner (next to your profile photo)
  2. You're signed out and returned to the home page

17. Troubleshooting & Common Scenarios

‚ùå "I can't see the Dashboard link in the navbar"

Why: You're not logged in, or you haven't been assigned a role.

Fix:

  1. Click "Staff Login" (blue button, top-right) ‚Üí Sign in with Google
  2. If still no Dashboard ‚Üí Ask the Super Admin to add your email at /admin/events/[eventId] ‚Üí Committee Members section

‚ùå "Access Denied" or "Unauthorized" page

Why: You don't have the right role for that page.

Fix: Ask Super Admin to assign the correct role. For example:

  • Teams page needs ‚Üí "Registration Committee" role
  • Jury page needs ‚Üí "Jury Admin" or "Jury Member" role
  • Logistics page needs ‚Üí "Logistics Committee" role

‚ùå "Team not found" on Team Portal

Why: Wrong phone number or team code.

Fix:

  • If using Phone: Enter the team lead's phone (not a member's)
  • If using Code: Check for typos ‚Äî codes look like IGN26-XXXX (uppercase)

‚ùå "I don't remember my team code"

Fix: Use the üì± Phone Number tab on Team Portal instead. Or ask the Registration Committee to look it up.

‚ùå QR Scanner camera not opening

Fix:

  1. Browser may ask for camera permission ‚Üí Click "Allow"
  2. If no camera available ‚Üí Use Manual Entry: type the coupon code in the text field and click "Verify"

‚ùå "Coupon already used"

Why: This coupon was scanned before (one coupon = one meal).

Fix: Check "Recent Scans" section to see when/who used it. Contact Super Admin if scanned by mistake.

‚ùå Jury member can't see assigned teams

Fix (check both):

  1. Jury Admin must assign teams first: /{eventId}/jury ‚Üí Jury Members card ‚Üí Assign Teams
  2. Super Admin must turn on evaluation: /admin/events/{id} ‚Üí Toggle "Evaluation Open" to ON

‚ùå Evaluation won't save

Fix:

  1. Make sure every question has a score (none can be blank)
  2. Try "Save Draft" first
  3. Check your internet connection

üîÑ Late Team Registration

  1. Super Admin: Toggle "Registration Open" back ON in event settings
  2. Team registers at /events ‚Üí picks the event ‚Üí fills the form
  3. OR Super Admin imports them via Excel at /{eventId}/import
  4. Registration Committee approves immediately

üîÑ Need to Change Team Member Details

  1. Team members can update food preference and accommodation in Team Portal
  2. For major changes: Registration Committee rejects the team, team re-registers with correct info, Committee re-approves
